Our Vision

NCOU envisions a self-sustaining generation of community members able to foster sustainable integrated development initiatives.

Our Mission

To promote, improve, and empower disadvantaged persons to become self-reliant through income-generating initiatives, formal elementary child education, skills development, community health initiatives, mindset behavior change, advocacy, and others.

Our Values

NCOU is committed to integrity, transparency, inclusivity, and collaboration in all aspects of its operations.

Our Impact

In 2023, NCOU collaborated closely with over 35,000 individuals, comprising 25,000 Nationals and 10,000 Refugees, providing support in areas such as agriculture, livelihood, education, health, and more.
